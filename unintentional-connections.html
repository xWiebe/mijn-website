Unintentional Connections

"Unintentional Connections" is part of a larger project called: "Dynamic Identity" by Fontys InnovationLab.

Dynamic Identity

The assignment given was to make the building interactive. We (students from Fontys InnovationLab) have researched different interaction techniques to create our own installations fitting the concept. The installation I have made is called "Unintentional Connections"

More about the project

The building's Identity

The first step in our process of creating these installations was to give the building an identity. After research we decided that the building has the following properties:

- Welcoming
- Inspiring
- Connecting
- Mindful

My contribution to Dynamic Identity: Emotional Dashboard

The property I had chosen for my interactive installation is 'Connecting'. I had to think about a concept that would touch this property. After some brainstorming I decided to visualise unintentional connections people have inside the builing.

When you're walking through the hallways of our building you will definitely find other people. Even though you're not talking to everyone you see, you are both aware of each other's presence. My goal with this interactive installation was to visualise this unintentional connection between the people inside TQ.
