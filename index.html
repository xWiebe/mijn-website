About Me

My name is Wiebe Abdoel, a student at Fontys InnovationLab. The past years I have been developing myself in the Media layer. My passion is the creation of concepts and the realisation of these concepts using ICT.

Currently I have my own company. I build websites and I am hired by Xerio as all-round ICT employee. My responsibility at Xerio is to solve all ICT related problems within the company. Because of this job I have developed my problem solving skills. I tend to think in solutions, not in problems. No matter the problem, there is always a solution!

I am enjoying my current job but my goal is to make a living by doing the things I love the most, that is why I am enrolled as student at the Fontys InnovationLab.
